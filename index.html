The word meme originated with Richard Dawkins’ 1976 book The Selfish Gene as a shortening for mimeme, a concept for discussion of evolutionary principles in explaining the spread of ideas and cultural phenomena.
The concept developed into internet memes, as we know them today, during the 90’s and early 2000’s. An Internet Meme is an activity, concept, catchphrase or piece of media which spreads, often as mimicry or for humorous purposes, from person to person via the Internet.
